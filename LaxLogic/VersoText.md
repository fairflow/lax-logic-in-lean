## Math Rendering

Verso supports LaTeX-style math:

Inline math: $x^2 + y^2 = z^2$

Block math:

$$
\forall \epsilon > 0, \exists \delta > 0, \forall x, |x - a| < \delta \implies |f(x) - L| < \epsilon
$$
